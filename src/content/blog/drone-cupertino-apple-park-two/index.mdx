I enjoyed experimenting with the top-down drone style shoots and trying out different elevation and angles. The dynamism is a significant improvement over the traditional camera - which was limited by your feet.

However, I would love to have the dynamic range of SLR and better camera control on the drone. Even with five bracketed shot, you probably only get the same dynamic range as a single full-frame SLR RAW. I await the day that we can have a full-frame drone camera.
