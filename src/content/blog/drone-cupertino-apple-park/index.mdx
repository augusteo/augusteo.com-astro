This flight was my first drone flight. My friend Alex was teaching me how to fly the drone and the camera options. It was fun but thrilling at the same time.

We left work around 4 pm and walked south to get outside of the SJO airport range as legally required by the FAA. Funnily, Apple Park was just on the edge of the airport range, so we didnâ€™t have to walk far.

I took all the pictures with five bracketed shot, which were then processed together with AuroraHDR and Luminar. I am quite impressed that the drone was stable enough to take long exposure shots. Considering that they are floating in the air.
